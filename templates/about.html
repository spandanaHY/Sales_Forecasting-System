System Overview
ARIMA Model
ARIMA (AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average) uses past values and errors to forecast future ones. We use ARIMA(5,1,0) — 5 lag terms, 1 differencing step, optimized for this retail dataset.
Superstore Dataset
Real-world retail data with 9,800 orders across 4 years (2015–2018). Includes Order Date, Category, Region, and Sales amount — aggregated to daily revenue for time series analysis.
Forecasting Engine
Choose 7 to 90 days of future predictions. Outputs include total forecasted revenue, daily breakdown, average, peak, and minimum values — all with confidence band visualization.
Visualizations
6 interactive graphs — daily sales trend, monthly revenue, time series decomposition (trend + seasonality + noise), actual vs predicted, error metrics, and 30-day forecast chart.
System Pipeline
